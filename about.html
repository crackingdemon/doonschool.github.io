About Doon Sainik School & Doon Military School

INTRODUCTION: Doon Sainik School and Doon Military School is a Preparatory Academy with an Education Partner, Lucent International, affiliated with the CBSE Board. Founded by Dr. Divya Soni, a distinguished Defence Officer, DSS offers coaching for Sainik School, Military School, RIMC, NEET, NDA, and IIT in the Super 30 Batch, based on Entrance Examinations. DSS is a pioneer in Defence Service Examination preparation, collaborating with ex-defence officers and defence institutions.

Our professional trainers prepare students to excel in various Defence Forces entrance exams, along with extensive experience in Judicial Services and IAS preparations. Admission to our Academy requires qualifying an entrance test assessing aptitude in Maths, English, General Studies, and Reasoning. Successful candidates undergo specialized training following a well-structured curriculum for guaranteed success.

We invite students to choose a rewarding career in Defence, Medical, and Engineering fields. Within one year, we provide comprehensive schooling, coaching, hostel facilities, and all essentials for success. DSS & DMS is a residential institution with integrated schooling, preparing students to crack RIMC, RMS, Sainik School entrance exams within a year, and NDA within two years.

Our holistic approach fosters physical, mental, and spiritual growth, emphasizing strength, skills, and true humanity. As a Co-educational English Medium school powered by Lucent International, we stand as the best school in Dehradun. Our curriculum integrates environmental awareness, community service, and global harmony. Our facilities include well-equipped classrooms, labs, libraries, and recreational spaces.
